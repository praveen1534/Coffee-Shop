LIGHT ROAST

196oc-205oc

Light roast is heated for a shorter time and at a lower temperature than dark roasts. The beans can reach a temperature of between 385 degrees and 410 degrees.

MEDDIUM ROAST

210oc-219oc

Medium roasted coffee is a compromise that often brings out the best flavors in coffees that have begun caramelization but have no burnt or chocolatey darkness

DARK ROAST

2250c-245oc

Dark roast coffee is a dark brown color and often has an oily surface. These coffees have a low acidity, heavy body, and tend to reveal deeper, darker flavorsCoffee
